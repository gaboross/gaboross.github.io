The COPP Strategy

Conscious Pilot Program

Let’s be honest: paragliding is an absolute addiction. The chemical rush of chasing thermals can cloud our judgment. COPP is our framework to ensure this obsession enriches our daily lives on the ground while keeping us fundamentally safe in the air.

1. Radical Danger Awareness

We completely strip away the romanticism. We explicitly study micro-meteorology, unexpected collapses, and human factor failures to step into our harnesses fully aware of the risk baseline.

2. Intentional Goals

We do not launch "just to fly." Every flight requires a conscious declaration of intent. Knowing exactly why you are in the air prevents you from overextending your limits when chasing adrenaline.

3. Life Enrichment

An addiction drains your life; a passion builds it. We utilize the focus, calm, and discipline acquired during extreme flights to transform ourselves into better parents, professionals, and friends on the ground.

Why "Anonymous"?

We are not truly hidden, but we embrace the humility of the name. In the presence of changing air masses and towering peaks, our egos must remain non-existent.

By checking our pride at the landing zone, we create a transparent safety culture where pilots can openly talk about mistakes, near-misses, and fear without facing mockery.
